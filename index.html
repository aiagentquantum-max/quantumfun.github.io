Blockchain Security

Blockchain networks face rising threats from both traditional hackers and the coming wave of quantum computers.

Quantum computers will soon be capable of breaking cryptographic algorithms such as ECDSA and RSA, which secure Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major blockchains.

  • Steal private keys
  • Forge transactions
  • Drain wallets and compromise cold storage within minutes

QuantumFun AI Agents act as autonomous guardians to protect blockchain ecosystems:

  • Monitor activity in real time to detect unusual or malicious transactions
  • Identify vulnerable wallets before attackers exploit them
  • Automatically migrate funds to post-quantum cryptography (PQC) protected addresses
  • Integrate with exchanges and protocols to freeze compromised accounts instantly

This proactive defense ensures a secure blockchain future, even as quantum computers grow powerful enough to threaten today’s cryptography.
